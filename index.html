Welcome to Nail Studio, a platform you can use to design your own nail art.

You can choose from different nail shapes and colors, as well as jewels and glitter.

If you choose to make your design a reality, you can get your nail art delivered to you!

With your order, you will receive a set of press-on/glue-on nail kit, with your design!

How it Works?

First, choose a nail shape and size.

Then decorate your nails using the available colors.

Once you are done, if you have created an account, you can save your nail designs to your profile.

If you want to purchase a set of nails with your design you may do so from your profile.

For your first order, you will receive a cute custom box containing your nails.
The box is large enough to store at least three sets of nails.
After your first order, you will receive your nail set in a bag designed to prevent your nails from chipping/breaking.
